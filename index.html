Brief

Cover Saudi and regional news the way I would actually want to read it. Modern, real-time, English-first, and faster than the traditional outlets.

What I made

Saudi Insider was created as a modern English-language digital news and updates platform focused on delivering fast, visually engaging, and audience-driven coverage across Saudi Arabia and the GCC. The platform became recognized for its modern storytelling style, creative news presentation, and strong audience engagement, reaching more than 28M+ audience reach across digital platforms. Alongside Saudi Insider, the Saudi United WhatsApp community rapidly grew to nearly 5,000+ members, becoming an active real-time update platform during major regional situations, including the Iran-US escalation period. During critical moments, we continuously shared live developments, civil defense alerts, interception updates, safety-related information, and regional coverage in a fast and organized format. This helped audiences stay informed through real-time digital communication and community-based media updates.

SYSTEM · SAUDI INSIDER BOT

News Curation Pipeline.

An AI-assisted newsroom that runs in the background. Source ingestion, relevance filtering, brand-voice rewriting, and WhatsApp delivery, all wired into one reliable workflow.

01 · Ingest Sources X monitoring, Telegram channels, RSS feeds, official accounts ›
02 · Filter AI Filtering Gemini relevance scoring, dedupe, thread stitching ›
03 · Rewrite Editorial Rewrite Claude brand-voice rewriting, translation, format clean-up ›
04 · Queue Posting Queue Retry-safe queue, scheduling, rate-limit handling ›
05 · Deliver WhatsApp Delivery WAHA broadcast, batched updates, daily digests & recaps
Project overview

Saudi Insider Bot is an automated news aggregation and distribution pipeline built to monitor, filter, enrich, and deliver Saudi-related updates around the clock. It works like an AI-assisted newsroom, turning scattered information from X, Telegram, RSS, and official sources into clean, branded WhatsApp updates.

Technical highlights
  • Multi-LLM strategy. Gemini handles relevance filtering at scale, Claude handles brand-voice rewriting where tone matters.
  • Async concurrency. Scraping, AI calls, and posting all run in parallel, keeping latency from source to broadcast low.
  • Resilience first. Retries, health checks, database pruning, and safe shutdowns keep the pipeline running unattended.

05   ARTWORK

Artwork & Creative
Beginnings.

Before the media work, there was art. A YouTube channel run from a desk during lockdown that quietly became the foundation for everything that came after.

During the COVID-19 lockdown, I started a YouTube channel called Ryanz Art as a space to share my drawings and practice visual storytelling.

What began as a personal hobby slowly became something more serious. It taught me consistency, patience, presentation, and the discipline of creating for an audience, even when everything started from a desk at home.

That same creative instinct eventually moved beyond paper into photography, videography, editing, and digital media. The tools changed, but the habit stayed the same: observe, create, publish, and keep improving.
